Committed to bring Art and CS together.

I am master student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor studing Human Computer Interaction (MSI).

My research interest lays in the intersection of Human Computer Interaction, AI and Vision & Graphics.

I am particularly interested in enabling creation and design with computational methods.

Currently I am working with Prof. Eytan Adar on interesting projects concerning Human AI Collaboration.

Before Joining UMSI, I graduated from the School of Computer Science, Fudan University where I was advised by Prof. Yang Chen. I was a member of the Big Data and Networking (DataNET) Group.
