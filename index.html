I am master student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor studing Human Computer Interaction (MSI).
My research interest lays in the intersection of Human Computer Interaction, AI and Vision & Graphics. I am particularly interested in enabling art design and creation with computational methods.

Currently I am working with Prof. Eytan Adar on interesting projects concerning Human AI Collaboration.

Before Joining UMSI, I graduated from the School of Computer Science, Fudan University where I was advised by Prof. Yang Chen. I was a member of the Mobile Systems and Networking (MSN) Group.
